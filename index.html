Advertising is Broken.

Find Out How

AdLyft is a team of innovators, visionaries, and doers who think big.

We are an ad-tech company that believes advertising on mobile should be less like detention, and more like a conversation.

It’s broken and we’re going to fix it.

We have built a software platform that facilitates the creation and distribution of incentivized, interactive, engagement based advertising within applications that users already have on their devices.

We verify the user actively participated in an advertisement and greatly reward them for giving us their undivided attention.

Brands, publishers, and users all win.

When we’re successful there will be a whole new way to monetize content that will generate entirely new business models for innovative content delivery including news, games, social, and more.
